Privacy Policy for TaleTap

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

Thank you for visiting TaleTap. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you access or use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register an account, post comments, or add novels to your bookshelf, we may collect your username, email address, or other contact details you voluntarily provide.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may collect anonymous data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referral source, and usage data through cookies or analytics tools.
  • GeoIP Data: We collect IP addresses to determine your geographic location for access control purposes, which may include restricting access from certain regions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the content and functionality of the site, including novel browsing, commenting, and bookshelf features.

  • Restrict access from specific regions to comply with our access policies.

3. Cookies

This website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us maintain user sessions, analyze web traffic, or recognize you when you return to the site.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features (e.g., login or bookshelf) may not function properly.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to track website performance. These providers may collect non-personal information through cookies or similar technologies.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to outside parties without your consent, except as required by law or to enforce access restrictions.
